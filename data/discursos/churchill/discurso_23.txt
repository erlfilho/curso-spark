Winston Churchill to Franklin D. Roosevelt (March 17, 1941)

MESSAGE FROM PRIME MINISTER FOR PRESIDENT DATED MARCH 17th 1941.
Pray accept my most sincere congratulations and grateful thanks on your magnificent speech. It is a trumpet call to free men all over the world, and will play a weighty part in drawing them together. I must also thank you for your most kind and complimentary reference to me.
